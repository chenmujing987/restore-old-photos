2. Photoshop

Verdict: Powerful but steep learning curve.

Adobe remains the industry standard. Its "Neural Filters" offer AI photo restoration, but it requires a monthly subscription and significant editing skills. For casual users looking for the best software to fix older photos, it may be overkill.

3. Remini

Verdict: Popular for selfies but expensive.

Great at sharpening faces, but often criticized for aggressive upselling and "fake" looking results on non-facial details. The free version is very limited.

4. MyHeritage

Verdict: Good for animating photos.

Known for its "Deep Nostalgia" feature. Excellent for genealogy enthusiasts but requires a generic account setup. Photo restoration quality is decent but not industry-leading.

Verdict: Good for desktop users.
