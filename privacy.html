Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how "FotoRevive" (the "App") and its associated website (collectively, the "Service"), created by Yanmei Chen, manages your information. By using the Service, you agree to the terms set out in this policy regarding the collection and use of your information.

1. Information Collection and Use

1.1 How We Collect and Process (Face Data in App)

Our App offers powerful AI-powered features, "Face Restoration" and "Animate." To enable these features, we process media files that you voluntarily provide. This section details the specific data involved, which may include face data.

  • For the "Face Restoration" Feature (powered by GFPGAN): When you use this feature, we process the digital photograph you select to analyze pixel data and enhance facial features.
  • For the "Animate" Feature (powered by Live Portrait): When you use this feature, we process source photographs and driving videos to provide motion data.

Important: We DO NOT collect or store biometric identifiers. We do not use this data to create a faceprint or geometric scan of your face, nor do we use it to personally identify any individual. The data is treated solely as an input for the requested one-time computational task.

1.2 How We Use and Share Your Information

The use of your media files and any face data contained within is strictly limited to providing the AI feature you have explicitly requested.

The Process:

  1. User-Initiated Action: You select your media files within the App.
  2. Secure Transmission: Your device securely transmits files via encrypted HTTPS to our third-party API provider, Replicate.com. This is the only context in which media files are shared.
  3. Ephemeral Cloud Processing: The AI model processes files temporarily in the server's RAM.
  4. Return and Deletion: The result is sent back to your device. Replicate.com deletes original files immediately after processing.

We solemnly swear that we will never use your media files for advertising, user profiling, or for training AI models.

1.3 Data Storage and Retention (Zero-Retention Policy)

  • Our Servers: We do not operate our own servers for media storage. We do not retain any of your files.
  • Third-Party Servers (Replicate.com): Files exist only temporarily for the duration of the processing task and are not retained.
  • Your Device: The final result is stored exclusively on your local device only when you choose to save it.

This Log Data may include your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, browser version, the pages of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and other statistics.
  • Cookies: Our website and App may use "cookies" to gather information and improve our services. You have the option to either accept or refuse these cookies. Please be aware that if you choose to refuse them, you may not be able to use some portions of our Service.

3. Log Data and Analytics (App)

We also use Google Analytics for Firebase to understand usage patterns (e.g., button clicks, screen views) to improve the App. This data is for internal analysis only and is not sold.
